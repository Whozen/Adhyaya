About Adhyaya

Adhyaya Institute formerly known as Pearl Academe Education is an institute established with the vision “To set a Standard of Learning and make a difference by empowering people”. We have been conducting various Academic-Aptitude and Professional courses since last 5 years. Adhyaya is specialized preparation center for Aptitude based Tests: GMAT, MBA Entrance Prep., EMBA Entrance Prep., MBM Entrance Prep., and MBA Admission Guidance. We “ Bridge the GAP between College Life and Corporate World” by providing Professional- Practical-Work Oriented courses like Accounting Training, Taxation, Advance MS Excel, General Office Management, Receptionist Training, and Entrepreneur Kits for Start-up.

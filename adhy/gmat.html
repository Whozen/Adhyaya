GMAT

Graduate Management Admission Test (GMAT) is a Test taken to get admission to MBA colleges worldwide in various Universities and Business Schools. GMAT is used to promote fairness, objectivity and validity in selection, and provides supplementary information to that contained in your application form for your MBA admission It DOES NOT test knowledge in specialized academic subjects, but provides an indication of the ability for clear and systematic thought
